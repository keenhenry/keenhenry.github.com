About Henry

Henry is a Master student at Columbia University working towards his degree in Computer Science. He was born in Taipei, Taiwan, but grew up in a city called Hsinchu. Before coming to Columbia, Henry obtained his BS degree in Electronics Engineering at National Chiao-Tung University, Hsinchu, Taiwan. In 2004, his parents moved to New Zealand, which is considered his second home now.

When he has free time, Henry loves to take pictures of landscape and people. Other than that, he watches baseball but plays basketball, table tennis and Go (A Chinese board game). Last but not least, he sometimes writes some little programs for fun.
